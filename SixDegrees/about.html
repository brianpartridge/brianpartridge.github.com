Six Degrees

$1.99

What are degrees of separation?

Degrees of separation are a way of identifying how closely two people are connected through associations with other people. Six degrees is the average separation between any two people on Earth! The app calculates degrees of separation between actors, using their work in movies, TV, and video games.

Discover connections between actors.

With the three different modes you'll find all sorts of connections between actors that you never knew existed. Maybe your leading man and leading lady are connected though works you've never heard of. Six Degrees can help to find these connections and introduce you to new works.

Test your geek credentials.

Play the trivia and guessing modes with friends to see who really knows the most minutia about pop culture.

Features

• Lookup degrees of separation between any two actors.

• Trivia mode, complete the degrees of separation.

• Guess Degrees mode, test your assumptions.

• Buy or rent movies right from iTunes.

• IMDb app and website integration.

• Netflix and Wikipedia searching.

• Some easter eggs for sci-fi geeks.
   See if you can find them.

Requirements

• Supported on iOS 4 and newer.

• A WiFi or 3G data connection.
