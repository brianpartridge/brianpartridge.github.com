Power Reader upgrade

$2.99

Become a power reader!

Purchasing the Power Reader upgrade through in app purchase enables extra functionality in LazyEye.

• Faster reading speeds

The non-Power Readers are limited to reading a maximum of 200 words per minute. The Power Reader upgrade enables reading up to 1000 words per minute.

open image

• Disable advertisements option

By default LazyEye presents non-obtrusive advertisements while reading. Upgrading enables an option to disable the display of these advertisements.*

open image

• Support independent software development

By upgrading you are supporting the furture development of LazyEye. New features, bug fixes, and improvements will all be possible because of your support.

* If you want to keep supporting development you can leave the ads on too.
