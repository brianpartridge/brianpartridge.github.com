What is Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP)?

RSVP is a way to read a large amount of text, in a limited amount of space, quickly. Typical reading involves moving your eyes over lines of text. RSVP reading involves focusing your eyes on a location where text is displayed one word at a time.

Make RSVP reading like watching a movie.

By providing an interface similar to the iOS movie player, can read long form text without the clutter of flashing advertisements. Navigation controls and progress bars behave just as you expect, allowing navigation of text a paragraph, sentence, or word at a time.

Don't lean forward, sit back.

Reading on a small screen can sometimes entail bringing the device up to your nose. With RSVP reading you only see one word at a time, so that word can take up the whole screen, and you can read from further away. Dock your phone on your desk and lean back, or use it on a treadmill and forget about losing your place like you would with the small text of a magazine.
